Change Old Habits

Among the many organizations in the Ateneo, Fash is a pro-active community that not only cares for the environment, but also the people. I believe the projects and interactions will help bring to light practices that do not depict a sense of responsiblity for the surroundings. Scroll down to find more reasons why I am excited to become a part of this community of environmnetally-conscious students.

Passion

I've always been interested in Fashion especially when it includes styling my friends and family.

Urgency

The fashion industry poses a large environmental problem. Ateneo doesn't even have uniforms so we can clearly see that we need to try to lessen the impact.

Collaboration

Being in a tech course, I don't meet a lot of people interested in Fashion. I want to meet new people, learn from them, and work on projects that we would all enjoy.

Influence

Sharing with other people what I now know is a good way to put the things I've learned to use. This way, I learn from them and they can learn from me too.

Experience

I plan, hopefully, to land a job that bridges the tech and fashion industries and I beleive having a sustainable perspective here is important.
